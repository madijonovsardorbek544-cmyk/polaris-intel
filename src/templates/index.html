The problem

Small organizations are expected to watch cyber threats, CVEs, ransomware campaigns, geopolitical disruptions, source failures, and sector risk every day. Most do not have analysts, threat intelligence platforms, or spare time to separate signal from noise.

They need a simple way to see what matters today, decide who owns the response, and prove what action was taken.

What POLARIS does

Monitors risk signals

Tracks cyber and geopolitical sources relevant to exposed teams and regions.

Scores and explains risk

Highlights severity, confidence, why it matters, and recommended next action.

Matches watchlists

Connects alerts to countries, sectors, organizations, CVEs, keywords, and threat actors.

Tracks response

Records owners, notes, status, resolution history, and value reports.

Why not just Google Alerts?

Google Alerts can notify you that words appeared online. POLARIS is built for operational risk response: it scores risk, matches alerts to watchlists, assigns owners, tracks response status, and produces customer proof reports that show what was monitored and what actions were recommended.
